About HBaker's

At HBaker’s, food isn’t just what we serve — it’s how we connect, how we care, and how we remember.

Our story began back in 2009 as Lucky Cafeteria, tucked away in the heart of Naif, Deira. We offered comforting meals made with love — the kind of food that reminded people of home. Over time, what started as a small eatery grew into a warm, welcoming hub for neighbors, friends, and families.

Today, we proudly reintroduce ourselves as HBaker’s – Your Daily Treat. Still in the same place. Still the same love. But now with a fresh new look, better service, and even more of your favorite dishes — all while keeping the spirit of our roots alive.

From spicy Kerala breakfasts and sizzling burgers to fresh juices and nostalgic pazhampori beef, we continue to serve memories on every plate.

Welcome to HBaker’s. Let every bite feel like home.
