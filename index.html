Our Story

Welcome to HBaker's Cafe

Founded in 2023, HBaker's Cafe has been serving authentic chai and snacks to the Dubai community. Our secret family recipes have been passed down for generations, ensuring every cup delivers an unforgettable experience.

We source only the finest ingredients from local suppliers, blending traditional methods with modern techniques to create the perfect balance of flavors in every sip.
