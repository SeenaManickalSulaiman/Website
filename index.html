Our Story

Founded in the heart of Dubai, HBaker's Cafe has been a sanctuary for tea lovers since 2010. Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve the most authentic chai experience outside of India.

Every cup at HBaker's is brewed with premium tea leaves, fresh spices, and prepared using traditional methods that have been perfected over generations. We take pride in our artisanal approach to tea-making.

What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our warm hospitality and unforgettable flavors.

- The HBaker Family
