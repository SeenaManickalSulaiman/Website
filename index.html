About Us

HBaker’s — A Neighborhood Favorite Since 2009

HBaker’s isn’t just a place to eat — it’s a place to belong. Since opening our doors as Lucky Cafeteria in 2009, we’ve welcomed our community with heartfelt meals and familiar flavors.

What began in the heart of Naif, Deira has grown into a trusted name, now reimagined as HBaker’s. We've kept the charm, the love, and the recipes — while refreshing the experience with a new name and a renewed promise.

Whether it’s our golden pazhampori with beef, our satisfying Kerala breakfasts, or vibrant juices and faloodas — every dish reflects a commitment to quality, comfort, and connection.

Step into HBaker’s and rediscover the joy of good food made with soul. We’re still your everyday treat — just better than ever.
