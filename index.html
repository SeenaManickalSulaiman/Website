About Us – HBaker’s (Formerly Lucky Cafeteria)

At HBaker’s, food is more than just a meal — it's a memory, a moment, and a connection.

We began our journey in 2009 as Lucky Cafeteria, quietly serving the heart of Naif, Deira with simple, homestyle dishes that made people feel at home. Over the years, we’ve built more than a business — we’ve built a bond with a community that grew with us, laughed with us, and returned not just for the taste, but for the warmth behind every plate.

Now, with a fresh look and renewed energy, we’re proud to step forward as HBaker’s – Your Daily Treat. Still in the same place, still guided by the same love — but with new ideas, improved service, and a commitment to celebrating both tradition and innovation.

From crispy snacks and flavorful burgers to Kerala breakfasts, pazhampori beef, fresh juices, and falooda — we serve with heart, because we know that food made with love is food that stays with you.

Welcome to HBaker’s. Where every bite feels like coming home.
